About

Beginnings

I was born and raised in New Jersey. Some people consider this a bad thing, even daring to call good old NJ "the armpit of the nation," but I've grown to appreciate my home. After living my life under one of the best public education systems in the country and not pumping my own gas, I fled the nest to Washington, DC. It's been pretty great so far.

Interests

In my free time, I like to bake, crochet, listen to podcasts, and write fiction. When I have even more time, I spend it reading and slowly chipping away at my TV watchlist. I like to pick up hobbies just to try my hand at anything - right now, I'm repainting old masterpieces. I just finished my rendition of "The Scream."

Currently reading: "Dune" by Frank Herbert
